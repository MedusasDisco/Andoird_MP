Medusa's Pixel

Medusa's Pixel was developed as a show case of what can be done with open source HTML5 game frameworks like PhaserJS and services like Fiverr to create a unique experience for fans. The psychedelic rock band, Medusa's Disco decided that since the current landscape of the music business changes daily, they should utilize creativity to try to stick out from the pack. Not only have they created a fun game, but they also got 6 of their songs converted over to chiptune to fully immerse the listener and user in a 8-bit Medusa's Disco experience.
